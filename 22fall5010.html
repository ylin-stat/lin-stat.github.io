STAT 5010: Advanced Statistical Inference, Fall 2022

This course provides graduate students with foundation of statistical inference. For more details, please also check the course outline on Blackboard.

  • Instructor: Prof. Tony Sit.

  • Topics:

    • Random Variables, Their Transformations and Expectations

    • Common Families of Distributions

    • Properties of Random sample

    • Principles of Data Reduction

    • Asymptotics/Large-sample Theory

    • Point Estimation

    • Hypothesis Testing and Interval Estimation
